About

We have been brewing beer in Bungay since 1991. Master brewer Robert Pickard creates a unique drinking experience for your de-energised palate.

Curry night every Wednesday from 6pm.

The Guardian recommends us in their Let's Move To Bungay article.

The Huffington Post says we may possibly be the best pub in the known-world.

Check out our Facebook group.

Beer

All Green Dragon beer is brewed on site from whole hops and local malt.

Chaucer Ale 3.7% ABV (challenger hops)

Cask-conditioned full flavoured hoppy session bitter.

Bridge Street Bitter 4.5% ABV (challenger hops)

Cask-conditioned strong hoppy bitter.

Dragon Gold 4.4% ABV (styrian golding hops)

Cask-conditioned golden ale with a citrus aroma.

Winter Warmer 6.5% ABV (styrian golding hops) - when available

Strong winter mild with seasonal flavours.

Other beers, wines, spirits, etc. also available.
