About us

The Guardian gushes about us in their Let's Move To Bungay article.

Our Beers

Our beers are brewed on site from whole dried hops and local malt.

Chaucer Ale 3.7% ABV (challenger hops)

Cask-conditioned full flavoured hoppy session bitter.

Bridge Street Bitter 4.5% ABV (challenger hops)

Cask-conditioned strong hoppy bitter.

Dragon Gold 4.4% ABV (styrian golding hops)

Cask-conditioned golden ale with a citrus aroma.

Winter Warmer 6.5% ABV (styrian golding hops) - when available

Strong winter mild with seasonal flavouring.
