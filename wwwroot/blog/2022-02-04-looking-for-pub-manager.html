LIVE IN PUB MANAGER WANTED

Published 4th February 2022, by James Pickard

⭐⭐ BIG NEWS ⭐⭐

We're on the lookout for a pub manager to head up our fabulous team and run our pub!

Since 1991 we've been brewing our own cask ale here in Bungay and our beer has formed the backbone of a thriving town pub.

This is an incredibly exciting opportunity for an experienced manager who's passionate about real ale and good beer in general as well as everything we're all about.

Come and join a close knit family business that's been an integral part of the community for the last 30 years and help shape a new chapter in our history.We've got exciting plans for the future and we'd like you to be part of it!

We offer a competitive salary with spacious self contained living accommodation above the pub.

Could this be the job for you? Drop an email to james@greendragonbungay.co.uk with your CV and a covering letter. We look forward to hearing from you!
